MrSmith is a web portal that serves as a launcher for several independent internal corporate mini-applications.

**Name & Theme:** Inspired by Agent Smith from The Matrix. The launch portal uses heavy Matrix-themed graphics (green code rain, dark aesthetic, terminal/cyber feel).

**Design philosophy:**
- Portal/launcher: Matrix-inspired visuals (dark theme, digital rain, glitch effects, monospace type)
- Individual mini-apps: high-level, polished design inspired by Stripe (clean, modern, excellent typography, whitespace, subtle animations)

**Why:** Internal corporate tooling, but with distinctive, high-quality design rather than generic admin UIs.

**How to apply:** When building the portal shell, lean into Matrix aesthetics. When building individual apps, switch to Stripe-caliber clean design. The two styles coexist — the portal is the dramatic entry point, the apps are the refined workspace.
